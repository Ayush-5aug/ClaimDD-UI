SNAPSHOT - TECHNICAL

ClaimDD is a Web Application that helps to evaluate the Delay and/or Disruption cost by just uploading the necessary data through an Excel file.


COST EVALUATION - DELAY AND DISRUBTION

ClaimDD evaluates cost shall be incurred (or incurred) due to the Delay and/or Disruption.

Thus, the cost claim for the Disruption can also be claimed.

Fact: One of the unidentified and/or unclaimed cost/expenditure is cost incurred due to Disruption. Solution: Use ClaimDD to identify (evaluate) and/or claim the disruption cost.

COST CLAIM REPORT

In addition to evaluate the cost, ClaimDD generates the Cost Claim Report.

The report shall justify the claimed cost. Thus, it can help the Claimant to persuade the cost claim effectively.

Fact: One of the reasons for the rejection of the cost claim is the lack of justification of the claimed headings. Solution: Use ClaimDD to present the cost claim with an appropriate professional justification report.

NINE CLAIMS HEADINGS

ClaimDD evaluates the cost under nine (9) claims headings such as:

1. Site Overheads;

2. Escalation,

3. Loss of Productivity,

4. Mobilisation,

5. Subcontractor's Claims,

6. Head office Overheads,

7. Damages,

8. Profit, and

9. Financial Charges.

The claim is generally entitled to most of the claims headings; pursuant to the Contract, the Claimant can choose the claims headings under which it has the right to recover from the counterparty.

Fact: One of the reasons for the time-barred claim is the failure to evaluate/estimate the cost that incurred and/or shall be incurred due to a delay/disruption event. Solution: Use ClaimDD to know all the expenditure (or likely expenditure) at appropriate time.

INSTANT CLAIMS

ClaimDD evaluates the delay and/or disruption cost that shall be incurred (or incurred) by the Claimant for any particular period (Event).

Thus, the cost claim can be prepared and submitted instantly.

Fact: The cost claim is mostly rejected (reduced) due to time-barred submission. Solution: Use ClaimDD to present the cost claim well in advance.

DATA MANAGEMENT

As a vast amount of data is involved, all data can be imported from the Excel file to ClaimDD within a minute. It avoids manual data entry operation.

TThus, the Quantum and/or Rate of staff, labour, material, work, etc., are uploaded from one page.

Fact:One of the reasons for the failure to make a claim is the vast amount of unorganised data. Solution: Use ClaimDD to organise the data in a way that can persuade the claim.

IMPACTED PERIOD

As each project may encounter various delay and/or disruption events, users can create any number of the events based on the event date and evaluate the cost impact for any of such events in just a click.

Fact:One of the main reasons for rejecting a cost claim is the failure to make a claim based on the impacted period. Solution: Use ClaimDD to make a claim based on the impacted period instead of extended period.

HEAD OFFICE OVERHEAD

Users can choose the formula for calculating head office overheads and loss of profit from the standard formula such as Hudson, Emde, and Echiley.

Further, Good Faith has introduced the amended Hudson formula and Emden formula.

Fact:One of the existing arguments is which formula should be used for calculating Head Office Overheads as Hudson and Emden formula has an error in accounting principle? Solution:Use ClaimDD to overcome such accounting principle errors, as Good Faith has corrected such errors and developed amended formulae.
