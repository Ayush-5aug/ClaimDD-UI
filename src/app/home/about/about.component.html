Constract Software Bureau

Good Faith – Contract Software Bureau – is a department of Good Faith (LLC). It develops web-based software ("Web Application") for simplifying contract expert tasks when administrating the construction project. Thus, Contract Party's representatives (who does not need to be an Expert) can complete the contract tasks such as evaluating cost and justifying the contractual rights like the experts. Moreover, the claim can be prepared and justified then and there. Thus, the Contract Party shall get an enormous benefit.

Good Faith believes it would pave the way for creating a universal platform for carrying out contract expert tasks. Thus, it would bring incredible benefits, mainly minimising the construction industry's conflicts and disputes.
