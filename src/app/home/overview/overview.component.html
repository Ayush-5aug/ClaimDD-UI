OVERVIEW

ClaimDD helps the contract party to Evaluate the Cost Impact and generate Claim Justification Report due to delay and/or disruption in the construction project.


ClaimDD helps the contract party to evaluate the cost (Cost Evaluation) under nine (9) claims headings and eighteen (18) cost headings. Further, it automatically generates claims justification report (Cost Claim Report). Moreover, the Cost Evaluation (and the Cost Claim Report) can be generated for any period based on the event's occurrence period for any number of Events. The complete Claim can be generated within an hour. Thus, it can help you to present the delay and/or disruption claim whenever needed.

WHY IT IS REQUIRED

In Construction projects, Delay and/or Disruption are unavoidable. A evaluating cost incurred (shall be incurred) due to delay and disruption requires expertness. Most of the time, the contract party may not have an expert when the delay and/or disruption event occurs. As a result, the contract party fails to submit the cost claim within a contract time limit, generally 28 days.

Though the Contract Party is entitled to be paid for such cost, most of the time, such claims are rejected based on the failure to submit the claim on time (within 28 days) that may lead to dispute too.

However, ClaimDD can help the Contract Party to overcome such challenges by evaluating such cost impact then and there and genereate Cost Claim Report for the submission. Thus, it maximises the success and nullify the disputes.

WHO CAN USE IT?

ClaimDD is data-driven Web Application. Thus, it can be used to evaluate and review the claim. Therefore, it can be used by the Contractor, Subcontractor, Claims Consultant, Consultant, and Client.
